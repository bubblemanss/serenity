Welcome to SafeSpace





This is a SafeSpace. Are you feeling a little bit anxious, nervous, or you just want to take a minute and go into another world? Then you have come to the right place.

  1. Put on the Oculus Rift.
  2. Relax.
  3. Go to the Form and write down your feelings.

With SafeSpace you will be able to ease into another atmosphere while expressing your feelings. SafeSapce will help you allow you to get in touch with your emotions, and will allow you to recognize the daily triggers that happen throughout your life overtime.
